ABOUT THE PROJECT

Built by students,
for students.

Placement Buddy is a Final Year Project that tackles one of the biggest challenges for engineering students — knowing the answers but failing the interview. We built an AI interviewer that actually trains you.

Our Mission
"To give every engineering student a fair chance at cracking placement interviews — through structured AI-powered practice that simulates real interview conditions."
❌

The Problem

  • Students study theory but don't practice speaking/writing answers under pressure
  • Mock interviews require scheduling, availability, and often cost money
  • YouTube videos don't give personalized feedback on your specific answers
  • Existing platforms test code output, not interview communication skills
  • No tool mimics the structured, one-question-at-a-time interview format
✅

Our Solution

  • AI interviewer that stays strictly in role — no casual chatting or off-topic tangents
  • Available 24/7, zero scheduling required, completely free to use
  • Real-time structured feedback on every single answer with ideal answers provided
  • Covers all three critical interview types: DSA, HR, System Design
  • Progressive difficulty that mirrors actual placement rounds precisely
PROJECT GOALS

What we set out to build

🎯

Bridge the gap

Most students know the theory but freeze in actual interviews. We fix that with structured practice.

🤖

Leverage AI

Use LLMs not as a search engine, but as a structured evaluator that mimics real human interviewers.

📈

Progressive learning

Build genuine confidence through progressively harder questions — not just random Q&A.

🆓

Free & accessible

Every student deserves quality interview prep, regardless of whether they can afford coaching.

DEVELOPMENT JOURNEY

How we built it

Phase 1
🔍

Research & Planning

Studied placement interview patterns across top companies. Identified three critical interview types for engineering students.

Phase 2
🧠

Prompt Engineering

Designed and iterated on the AI system prompt to produce structured, consistent, interviewer-style feedback every time.

Phase 3
🎨

UI/UX Design

Built a dark-themed, terminal-inspired interface that feels professional yet approachable for students under pressure.

Phase 4
⚙️

Development & Testing

Implemented the full app with routing, multi-mode chat, and real API integration. Thoroughly tested across devices.
